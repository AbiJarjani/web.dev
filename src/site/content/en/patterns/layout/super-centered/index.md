Use `place-contents: center` to center an element within its parent.

```html
---
patternId: super-centered
---

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
  <head></head>
  <div class="parent">
    <div class="box" contenteditable="">
      :)
    </div>
  </div>
</html>
```
